About Sky Freighters S.A.

Global Logistics Solutions

Mozambican company providing integrated global logistics and freight forwarding solutions since 2023.

Sky Freighters, S.A. (SKF) is a Mozambican company dedicated to integrated global logistics and freight forwarding solutions, founded in 2023. Our operations focus on efficiency, transparency, and innovation.

We identified an underexplored opportunity in the Mozambican market: providing differentiated global logistics solutions that meet international needs competitors cannot fulfill.

Our Global Commitment: Clear processes, door-to-door planning, and worldwide cargo consolidation. We ensure tracking and risk management with defined Service Level Agreements (SLA) and maintain a global partner network.
